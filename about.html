OUR LEADERSHIP

Pratiksha Renake

Head of Design

Pratiksha Renake leads our design team with creativity, precision, and a deep understanding of space and aesthetics.

She holds a Master's in Architecture (MArch) from University College London (UCL) and brings extensive experience in delivering high-end residential projects from concept to completion.
